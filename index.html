HOW TO PLAY

THE CONCEPT

Guide the Bird and Whale to meet at the Water Line. Touch anywhere - they both move toward your finger. Land the Bird on the Whale's back to score points and advance levels!

CONTROLS

TOUCHDrag anywhere. Both animals follow.

MOUSEMove cursor to guide.

KEYBOARDArrow keys to steer.

SCORING & LEVELS

  • Land on whale: +100 points
  • Each level: Faster drain, more hazards
  • Survive as long as possible!

SURVIVAL

  • Bird needs FLIGHT ENERGY - recharges on whale
  • Whale needs BREATH - recharges at surface
  • When together at Water Line, BOTH recharge
  • Avoid black hazards and wind gusts!
